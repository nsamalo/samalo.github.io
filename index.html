    Who am I?

    “Who am I?”

    A question that has been rushing through every human being, conciously or not. In this post we will dive deep into our reality.

    The answer to that question is; we are life.

    Photo by Mohamed Nohassi on Unsplash

    “Stop acting so small. You are the universe in ecstatic motion.”
    ― Rumi

    We are Spiritual Beings living in a sack of meat that serves as our spaceship for the mission we’re on earth for. But what are we to become after Earth? That is another question we ask ourselves, this topic is full of questions, you get one answered and there comes another question, just like mathematics(oops, did I just mention mathematics?) “Oh there we go again, just what exactly is life, tell us and let us get it over with already!” Life is wind, it comes and goes, it rests not, it dances, it rejoices. It sees no barrier whatsoever, it needs only one passport to be well for a whole eternity, and that passport is the soul of life and the soul of life is known as Yeshua HaMashiach(the Anointed One).
